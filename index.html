OUR STORY

A Place Where Pets Come First

Paws & Play was founded in 2015 by Sarah Mitchell, a certified master groomer with a vision to create a stress-free environment where pets could receive top-quality care.

What started as a small grooming salon has grown into Austin's premier pet care facility, offering grooming, daycare, and boarding services for dogs and cats of all sizes.

Our team of certified professionals treats every pet like their own, using gentle handling techniques and positive reinforcement to ensure a happy experience.
